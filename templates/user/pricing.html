Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime? +
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately. If you upgrade, you get instant access to premium content.
Is there a free trial for paid plans? +
Yes — all new accounts get a 7-day free trial on any plan. No credit card required during the trial period.
What happens to my watchlist if I downgrade? +
Your watchlist is always saved. However, premium content will be locked and shown with a lock icon until you re-upgrade to the required plan.
Can I share my account? +
Standard plan supports 2 simultaneous screens, Premium supports 4. Each person in your household can have their own profile.
